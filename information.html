What would be your top three priorities if elected?

My top 3 priorities if elected would be to continue to build strong relationships with our current business owners, to support our school district, which is the heart of our city, and finally to help foster a city-wide environment where our citizens’ voices are heard. 

A Steadfast Commitment to Affordable Housing

Luqman is pushing for affordable housing, proposing plans to increase housing stock, regulate rent, incentivize affordable development, protect tenant rights, and encourage homeownership. [Your Name] calls for residents' active support to turn the promise of affordable housing into a reality for all in the city.

Uniting Against the Opioid Crisis: A Call to Protect Our Youth and Young Adults

At the same time urgently addressing the opioid crisis impacting the youth and young adults of our community. Advocating for comprehensive intervention strategies, Luqman supports increased access to education about the dangers of opioids, more treatment facilities, and community-based support systems. Recognizing that this is a crisis we cannot fight alone, the Councilmember encourages collective city-wide action and community support to protect and empower our vulnerable young people.

Support for Employment Proposals in Bloomington: Steps to Assess Impact and Define Policy Exceptions

Bloomington has always been a great city to earn a wage. I would support the Economic Development Authority efforts in this area to attract employers who share the same views to good paying jobs and be ready to stand behind the city staff as it is implemented in our business community.
